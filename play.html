Yogesh Naidu Mahareddy snips a play

THE EXCHANGE BY ALTHEA THURSTON

IMP

[_Throws down his pen._]

Your honor, I've all the miseries listed to date and a fine.

JUDGE

[_Scowls and impatiently taps the letter he is reading._]

Here is another one.

IMP

We have a defective heart or a lazy liver that we could give her.

JUDGE

She would not be satisfied.

IMP

Here comes another misery.

POOR MAN

Is that him?

IMP

Yes, that is his honor.
